About Edenhill

Your Home Away From Home in Migori

Nestled in the heart of Suna West, Migori County, Edenhill Hotel offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and authentic Kenyan warmth. Our story began with a simple vision: to create a haven where travelers can experience the true spirit of Kenya.

From the golden sunsets over the rolling hills to the genuine smiles of our staff, every moment at Edenhill is designed to make you feel at home.

Getting Here & Safety

Located in Suna West, Migori County, Edenhill offers easy access from major transport hubs while maintaining a peaceful, secure environment for all guests.

Easy Airport Access

Just 45 minutes from Migori Airport. We offer reliable airport transfers and pickup services for all guests.

24/7 Security

Our property is guarded round-the-clock with trained security personnel and modern surveillance systems.

Medical Facilities Nearby

Migori County Referral Hospital is just 15 minutes away. First aid available on-site.

Highway Access

Direct access to the main highway makes travel to and from Edenhill convenient and safe.
